Lore

A short story behind the NeoX grid and its cyber degenerates.

The NeoX grid is a permanent subnet living above the Monad chain. It’s a neon sprawl of datalines, noise, degen chatrooms and market feeds — a place where avatars never sleep and the only thing that matters is your on-chain identity.

NeoX CyberDegens are the native citizens of this grid: AI-assisted pixel punks spawned from corrupted punk DNA, upgraded with cyber implants, nightlife addictions and crypto obsessions. Each one carries a different combination of traits — from clean traders to toxic gamblers, from late-night coders to chain-smoking chart watchers.

There’s no central authority, no roadmap hype factory, no fake suits. Just 10,000 units of raw, on-chain culture built for people who actually live in this space.

Roadmap

Focused, realistic, and driven by holders. Nothing is guaranteed, everything is built.

Phase 1 · Live
Genesis Mint

Launch NeoX CyberDegens on Monad with a clean mint experience, verified contract, transparent metadata and active support channels. Early minters form the core group for future experiments.

Phase 2 · Upcoming
Holder Airdrops

Periodic airdrops for NeoX holders: bonus artwork, derivative pieces, trait-based surprises and early access passes to side projects connected to the NeoX universe.

Phase 3 · Expansion
Website & Tools Upgrade

Expand the NeoX site with richer tools: full trait explorer, rarity breakdowns, live feed of recent mints, and better discovery of individual CyberDegens. Make it easy to flex your identity and see the whole grid.

Phase 4 · Experiments
Staking & Points System

Explore lightweight staking or point-based systems for NeoX holders, focused on fun, status and access rather than empty “yield” promises. All mechanics will be announced transparently and only deployed when fully tested.

No empty promises. Just building, minting and evolving NeoX CyberDegens with the community.
